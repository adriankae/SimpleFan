#Simple fan 


## Description 

A simple fan out of spare parts for hot summer days and nights.

*Date: 02.06.2016 (dd/mm/yyyy)*

## Diffiuclty

Very easy.

Requires moderate soldering skills.

## Parts list

- two 12V fans
- 12V power supply
- 2 wooden bar
- screws
